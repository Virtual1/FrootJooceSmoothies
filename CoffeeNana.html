Coffee and Banana

Back to Index

Ingredients


  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • 1 banana (large)
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 cup almond milk (or regular milk)
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon honey(optional)

Directions

  1. Pour the coffee into ice cube tray and freeze for 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Place the frozen coffee ice cubes and the rest of the ingredients into the blender.
  3. Pulse until smooth and frothy
