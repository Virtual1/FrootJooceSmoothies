Banana Blues

Back to Index

Ingredients


  • 2 Bananas (cut into chunks and frozen)
  • 1/2 cup skim milk
  • 2 1/4 cups frozen blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt

Directions

  1. In a blender, combine all the ingredients.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
