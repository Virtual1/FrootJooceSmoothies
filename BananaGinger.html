Banana Ginger Dream

Back to Index

Ingredients


  • 1 banana, sliced (large)
  • 3/4 (6oz) vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Directions

  1. Combine the banana, yogurt, honey and ginger.
  2. Blend until smooth.
