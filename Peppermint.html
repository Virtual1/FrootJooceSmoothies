Peppermint

Back to Index

Ingredients


  • 6 ounces greek yogurt (plain)
  • 1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 6 mint leaves (torn into pieces)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 drops mint extract (optional)
  • sweetener (your choice, optional)
  • sanding sugar (optional)

Directions

  1. Put a thin layer of honey on the rim of the glass and dip into sanding sugar. Set glass aside.
  2. In a blender, add yogurt and almond milk, and mix together. Add mint, vanilla, optional extract and sweetener. Blend it up! Enjoy.
